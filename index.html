Welcome to Creon Campus

One app for all your campus needs!

Creon Campus is an all-in-one Android application designed to simplify your campus life. With a variety of features, this app serves as a central hub for diverse activities and services at IIT Madras. Below are the main features available on the app:

Isthara

Isthara offers a variety of shops for self-pickup, delivery, and dine-in services. Explore and manage your orders seamlessly.

Explore Isthara

Zaitoon

Order your favorite dishes from Zaitoon with convenient home delivery options directly through the app.

Order from Zaitoon

Insti OLX

Buy, sell, or exchange items within the IITM community. With secure Google login via Firebase, you can manage your listings effortlessly. Data is securely stored in Firestore.

Go to Insti OLX

Mess Coupons Booking

Book your mess coupons online with ease and enjoy your meals without hassle.

Book Mess Coupons
